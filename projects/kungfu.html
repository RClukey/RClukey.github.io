Kung-Fu

I have been participating in Kung-Fu since 2009. Throughout the years, I have progressed in belt levels until reaching the 4th-degree black belt that I am today.



As I 4th-degree, I have taught the lower belts and younger students techniques to survive in hand-to-hand combat. As technology has advanced, the need for hand-to-hand combat skills has decreased, however the skills that Kung-Fu provides are essential in living a balanced life.



Kung-Fu provides enormous health benefits in the form of exercise and mental stimulation. Kung-Fu has also taught me dedication and persistance despite various obstacles that follow. These are just a few of the skills that Kung-Fu has taught me, and I can't wait for the next class.
